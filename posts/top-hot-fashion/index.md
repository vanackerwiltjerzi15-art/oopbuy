Street style photography and fashion influencers provide real-time data on what's actually being worn by style-conscious individuals. Tracking these trends helps you stay current without following fleeting micro-trends.

- **The Formula:** Focus on items that appear consistently across multiple street style sources and influencer posts, indicating genuine trend momentum.

The best-dressed individuals don't just follow trends—they anticipate them. Understanding trend cycles helps you position yourself ahead of the curve.
